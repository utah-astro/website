Undergraduate Program in Astronomy and Astrophysics

the astro group — department of physics and astronomy

 

The University of Utah offers both a minor and a Physics major with an astrophysics emphasis. Here are some resources if you're interested in learning some astronomy:
  • The Astrophysics and Astrophysics Emphasis requirements and Astronomy Minor requirements from the University General Catalog. The full list of Physics & Astronomy courses is given here.
  • The Department Undergraduate Handbook, which has both the degree requirements for the minor and astrophysics emphasis.)
  • Note that PHYS/ASTR 2500 is the "gateway" course to our astronomy course offerings -- this is offered each fall; other courses may be offered only every two years. Please talk to us if you need help planning your major.
